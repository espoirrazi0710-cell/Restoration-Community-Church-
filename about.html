About Restoration Community Church

A Christ-centered community devoted to biblical teaching, fellowship, the breaking of bread, and prayer.

Who We Are

Restoration Community Church is a Christ-centered church committed to restoring lives, families, and communities through the transforming power of Jesus Christ.

We believe in teaching the Word of God with clarity and truth, building loving fellowship among believers, and living out our faith through prayer, discipleship, and service.

Our History

Restoration Community Church was founded by Pastor Banza Mukalay, who, together with his family, came to the United States as refugees from a refugee camp in Tanzania in 2012.

What began as a small prayer fellowship has grown into a vibrant church community focused on spiritual growth, unity, and outreach to all nations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see lives restored, believers equipped, and communities transformed through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

We strive to raise disciples who live out their faith boldly, walk in love, and impact the world for God’s glory.

Mission & Core Values

Mission Statement

Restoration Community Church exists to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through biblical teaching, loving fellowship, prayer, and the breaking of bread, empowering believers to live transformed lives and impact their communities with the Gospel.

Our Core Values (Acts 2:42)

“They devoted themselves to the apostles’ teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer.” — Acts 2:42

1. Biblical Teaching

We are devoted to the Word of God as the foundation of our faith.

2. Fellowship

We value genuine Christian community united in Christ.

3. Breaking of Bread

We honor communion and shared meals as expressions of unity.

4. Prayer

We are a praying church, fully dependent on God.
