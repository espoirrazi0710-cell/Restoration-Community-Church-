Mission & Core Values

Mission Statement

Restoration Community Church exists to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through biblical teaching, loving fellowship, prayer, and the breaking of bread, empowering believers to live transformed lives and impact their communities with the Gospel.

Meet Our Pastor

He is originally from the Democratic Republic of Congo and, together with his amazing wife Nicole and their family of four children (three girls and one boy), came to the United States as refugees in 2012 from a refugee camp in Tanzania.

Pastor Banza carries a strong passion for the Gospel, discipleship, prayer, and building a Christ-centered community rooted in biblical truth and love. His life and ministry reflect God‚Äôs power to restore, redeem, and transform lives through Jesus Christ.
